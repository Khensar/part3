 History: Adrenaline seeker launched in 2018 by a team of outdoor loving friends. It 
only started with a blog and gear reviews, grew into an e-commerce. platform with 
an adrenaline driven focus.  
❖ Mission and vision: Our mission is to empower outdoor enthusiasts to  
explore, connect, and thrive in nature through quality gear and community.  
❖ Vision: We see a future where access to nature is not limited to your television 
Screen, gear, knowledge or isolation. Where sustainability is a standard not an 
upgrade. Where the line between solo explorer and thriving, community 
disappears on trial. We plan to be the most trusted name at the intersection of 
quality gear. From a first-time hiker to a seasonal alpinist, we want to be the reason 
more people go outside and chase that adrenaline.  
❖ Target audience: Outdoor enthusiasts (20-45 years old) that value adventure, 
sustainability, and community. From hikers to climbers, paddlers to snow sports 
fans-We gear you up for each memory.
